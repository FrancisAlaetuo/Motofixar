Welcome To Motofixar

Motofixar is a platform that keeps people in need of automobile diagnosis and servicing connected to trained and qualified technicians wherever they are, and whenever they need.
You no longer have to scratch your head for not having the contact details of your preferred technician in a given location, because, with Motofixar, your preferred technician is everywhere you go, and just one click away!

When Was Motofixar Conceived?

Motofixar was founded in 2024 by a team of developers driven by the dream of bringing into reality, the possibility of car owners and everyone else alike having real-time access to technicians round the clock in any location.

Our Mission

Driven by the need to have a functional platform that helps put an end to countless people being stranded on different terrains in different parts of the world due to faulty vehicles, our mission could not have had a greater propelling force to yield success.

Our aim is to ensure that more people have access to skilled technicians when and where they need them, ensuring that people do not stand the risk of being assaulted, or vandalised when their cars break down as is the current trend in most cases

For a long time, there has been a believe that car owners only get to have access to one good technician at a time; this technician is mostly located where the car owners live or work. Hence, whenever these car owners are not in the area where their preferred technician is located, it becomes very difficult to have access to other qualified technicians, in the area, for their car diagnosis, repairs and maintenance.

With Motofixar, car owners will now stay connected to technicians round the clock. This ensures that users of Motofixar do not have to worry about a deflated wheel in the middle of the bridge around 1:30AM, or an overheating vehicle or stiff crankshaft around 6:30PM as they are returning from work.

Be it for an emergency repair or for a routine vehicle maintenance, you can always trust Motofixar to connect you to a qualified technician in real-time.

Car Owners And Technicians In One Place

We provide a community where car owners in need of services and the skilled technicians who render the services create an account with us and can access their respective dashboard to monitor their history on the platform.

You Help Us Keep The Best Technicians On The Platform

At Motofixar, the power is completely in your hands!
With a review/rating system in place where users get to rate the quality of services rendered by a technician upon completion of a repairs appointment scheduled on the platform, technicians who receive the highest ratings from users, over time, will be recommended more to users in their locations, while technicians who receive the lowest ratings from users, over time, will be less likely recommended to users in their location, and will be subsequently delisted from the platform.

Our Vision

Every idea intended to resolve a big problem starts from a moderate scale and hopes to reach a wider audience who will benefit from the solution offered.

Our dream is to spread to 20 countries of the world across Africa, Europe, America and Asia in 2030. We want to be able to provide our services to people of different cultures, religions and background.

Africa is predominantly hit with a rise in the number of faulty vehicles each day due to the deplorable nature of most road networks. This brings the number of people in dire need of real-time technician support to an all-time high.
Motofixar intends to, especially, bridge that gap in the African region through our services.

Why Trust Motofixar?

We are driven by the need to see users have access to qualified professionals to ensure that they get the best services they deserve.
Your satisfaction is our priority as we are constantly working to ensure that the platform is improved with the best innovations to keep up with advancing technology.
