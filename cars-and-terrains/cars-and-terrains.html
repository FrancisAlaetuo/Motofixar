cars and their best-suited terrains

get informed knowledge of cars and roads!

Let's help you decide on the best car for you. Through our detailed piece on cars and their best-suited roads, we help you make a choice on what cars to go for based on the topography, road networks, and the climatic conditions of your area.

The different car types and their best utility conditions are contained below:

Hatchback

Suitable for hilly terrains

Convertible

Suitable for level and less busy roads

Coupe

Suitable for terrains with less traffic

SUV

Best for highways and heavy traffic roads

Sedan

Suitable for highways and bad roads

Sports Car

Best for highways, fast lane and level roads

Crossover

Suitable for highways, bad roads and hills

Station Wagon

Suitable for less busy roads

Minivan

Suitable for road trips and vacations

Pickup Truck

Best for offroad, hills and bad roads

City Car

Suitable for road trips and less busy roads

Hybrid

Best for highways and fastlane

Electric Cars

Suitable for fast roads and highways

Utility Vehicles

Suitable for hills, offroad and bad roads

4X4

Best for hills, offroad, swamps and bad roads

Roadster

Suitable for roads with less traffic

Compact Cars

Suitable for level roads

CUV

Suitable for roads with less traffic

Luxury Cars

Best for level roads with less traffic

Subcompact

Suitable for level roads with less traffic

Microcars
