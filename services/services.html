At Motofixar, we provide users with access to their car needs, ensuring that they stay driving safely and most conveniently.

Our services range from:

Connecting Users To Technicians: We bring users closer to skilled technicians, providing them with solutions to the car
maintenance and repairs needs. Through our platform, users can always schedule an appointment with technicians wherever
they are and whenever they want.

Automobile Diagnosis At Your Fingertip: With Motofixar, users will be able to run a diagnosis of their vehicle to test for
fault(s) and schedule a maintenance or repairs appointment where necessary.

Get Access To Product Stores Near You: Never get stuck with outdated car products, or get stranded by faulty car parts
or exhausted lubricants. We grant you access to product stores closest to you, wherever you may be! All you have to do is
turn on your location and you will have these services at your disposal.

Get Basic Car Maintenance Tips And Car Hacks: We provide users with firsthand information on how to run basic car
maintenance to ensure the optimum utilisation of theirs vehicles. Not every car issue requires a visit to a technician.
With Motofixar, you get access to 'Do It Yourself' hacks that will provide you with the knowledge of how to fix minor faults
in your car, keep it running and from getting you stuck at a place while journeying.

Get Informed On The Best Usage Of Your Car: Every car has its most suited terrain where it gives the best performance
over a prolonged period. Our platform enlightens users hoping to get a new car or keep the existing one optimally running
on how best to get the best out of their cars by ensuring the ply the routes best suited for their type of car.

Technicians Gain More Patronage, Improving The Economy: Through our platform, a greater number of skilled
technicians are afforded more reach and visibility, playing a key role in keeping the economy growing. We ensure that the
best performing technicians, based on users ratings after service rendering are rewarded with more reach and visibility.
This is what sets us apart from the rest as users and the skilled technicians know they can trust us with their car needs.
Technicians, on the other hand, are motivated to render the best long-term service to users as that is what keeps them in
the platform.

Monitor Your Diagnosis And Maintenance History: Users can create an account with us where they get to monitor their
usage history in terms of diagnosis, maintenance and repairs. The same goes for the skilled technicians registered on our
platform as their overall ratings and performance index over any given period will be displayed on their dashboard.

Get Connected To Users Within Your Location: With our community and social media platforms, users can easily connect
with other users within their location ensuring that the Motofixar community keeps growing. We prioritise user happiness
and strive to build a community everyone will be proud to identify with.
